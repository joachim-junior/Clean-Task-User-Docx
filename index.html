How to Add a Project Team #back to top

Adding a Team for a project on the platform, is pretty much easy.
Please just follow along with the steps below.

1. Click on the + sign beside the text 'Teams' on the top left corner, below the navigation menu
2. A modal will appear with an input field asking you to give a name to the team you want to create.Fill in the name and click the create button.
Congratulations! you just added a team for your project.

How to Create a Project #back to top

Adding a project on the platform, is pretty much easy.
Please just follow along with the steps below.

1. On the home page, Click on the team for which you want to add a project.
2. Once you click, you'll be redirected to a new page. On this page, Click on 'Projects' tab.
3. Click on create a new board a modal will appear asking you to give a project title. Fill in your project title.
4. Once you click, you'll be redirected to a new page. That's it, you just created a project.
Congratulations! you just added a project.

How to Assign or Add Members to a Team #back to top

Assigning or adding members to a project on the platform, is pretty much easy.
Please just follow along with the steps below.

1. On the home page, Click on the team for which you want to add assign a member project.
2. Once you click, you'll be redirected to a new page. On this page, Click on 'Members' tab.
3. Click on 'INVITE MEMBERS' a modal will appear asking you to enter the email address of the person you want to add. Fill in the email.
4. Click on 'ADD TO TEAM'. Once you click, an email address will be sent to the person. When the person confirms the email, He will be automatically added to the project.
Congratulations! you just added a member to a project..
NB* To assign a member to a project, Just click on the empty input field, you'll see a list of all members available on the platform.

Please adding a member entails adding someone who is not already registered on the platform.
Assigning a member, means adding a someone who is already registered on the platform.

What is a Project State and How Do I Add a Project State #back to top

What is a project state ?

Like the name goes, a project state is simply Project State is a label applied to a project when it reaches a certain stage in its life cycle, such as when a project enters a new phase of development or passes a milestone in the approval process.
Project States are listed in approximately sequential order, so the list of associated Project States provides a sort of road map of the project development process. When a project enters the next phase of its life cycle, an administrator or Project Recorder assigns the next appropriate Project State to the project. Over time, the project is assigned a series of Project States to mark its progress from proposal to active development to approval and publication.

How to add a project state

1. Click on the + button at the bottom left hand corner.
2. A modal will show up asking you to enter a project state title. Fill in the title and click on the 'ADD STATE' button
Congratulations! you just added a state to your project

Please take note... To add a task to a state, click on the + button on the state card.
