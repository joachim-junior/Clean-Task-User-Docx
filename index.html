Step 3: Database Configuration

Create a database and Import the file cleantask.sql into the database. After that, Open the file found at application/config/database.php and provide your database configurations.

                                $db['default'] = array(

How to Add a Project Team #back to top

Adding a Team for a project on the platform, is pretty much easy.
Please just follow along with the steps below.

1. Click on the + sign beside the text 'Teams' on the top left corner, below the navigation menu
2. A modal will appear with an input field asking you to give a name to the team you want to create.Fill in the name and click the create button.
Congratulations! you just added a team for your project.

How to Create a Project #back to top

Adding a project on the platform, is pretty much easy.
Please just follow along with the steps below.

1. On the home page, Click on the team for which you want to add a project.
2. Once you click, you'll be redirected to a new page. On this page, Click on 'Projects' tab.
3. Click on create a new board a modal will appear asking you to give a project title. Fill in your project title.
4. Once you click, you'll be redirected to a new page. That's it, you just created a project.
Congratulations! you just added a project.

How to Assign or Add Members to a Team #back to top

Assigning or adding members to a project on the platform, is pretty much easy.
Please just follow along with the steps below.

1. On the home page, Click on the team for which you want to add assign a member project.
2. Once you click, you'll be redirected to a new page. On this page, Click on 'Members' tab.
3. Click on 'INVITE MEMBERS' a modal will appear asking you to enter the email address of the person you want to add. Fill in the email.
4. Click on 'ADD TO TEAM'. Once you click, an email address will be sent to the person. When the person confirms the email, He will be automatically added to the project.
Congratulations! you just added a member to a project..
NB* To assign a member to a project, Just click on the empty input field, you'll see a list of all members available on the platform.

Please adding a member entails adding someone who is not already registered on the platform.
Assigning a member, means adding a someone who is already registered on the platform.

What is a Project State and How Do I Add a Project State #back to top

What is a project state ?

Like the name goes, a project state is simply Project State is a label applied to a project when it reaches a certain stage in its life cycle, such as when a project enters a new phase of development or passes a milestone in the approval process.
Project States are listed in approximately sequential order, so the list of associated Project States provides a sort of road map of the project development process. When a project enters the next phase of its life cycle, an administrator or Project Recorder assigns the next appropriate Project State to the project. Over time, the project is assigned a series of Project States to mark its progress from proposal to active development to approval and publication.

How to add a project state

1. Click on the + button at the bottom right hand corner.
2. A modal will show up asking you to enter a project state title. Fill in the title and click on the 'ADD STATE' button
Congratulations! you just added a state to your project

Please take note... To add a task to a state, click on the + button on the state card.

    How to add Notes #back to top

    Notes can serve as a useful way to write a down a brainstorm or an idea, to add a note;
  • Click on the top right button “Notes”.
  • On the new page that appears, click on the bottom right button with the plus sign on it. A pop up will appear with various fields to help you customize your note – it is even possible to select the background colour of the note to be created.

    • How to upload a file #back to top

    Clean task provides the possibility of uploading a file to the platform in the directory of your choice. This is quite useful because it gives you the ability to safeguard documents of your choice, hence providing a reliable backup option for your files. To upload a file,
  • Click on the top right button “Media Library”.
  • On the new page that appears, navigate to the directory (folder) of your choice and click on the first button found in the navigation bar
  • On the pop up the appears click on “add files” and select the files to be uploaded and click “start upload”.
    • Note: Only a maximum of 5 files can be uploaded simultaneously.

    How to delete a folder/media#back to top

    To delete a previously uploaded file or folder,
  • Click on the top right button “Media Library”.
  • On the new page that appears, navigate to the directory (folder) of your choice and select the files/folder you want to delete by clicking on the top-right checkbox that appears on a file/folder when hovering on it.
  • After the files have been selected, click on the delete button found on the navigation bar.

    This is an area where a set of articles previously added by the administrator of the platform can be accessed. Here, it is possible to read an article and give a feedback, saying whether the article was helpful or not. In order to read an article found in the knowledge base,
  • Click on the top right button “Knowledge base”.
  • The new page that appears consist of the various knowledge base categories. Select a category by clicking on the “view article” button that appears when hovering on it.
  • A category can be made up of 0 or several articles. To read any of the articles found in a category, simply click on the article.

    How to Add a New User #back to top

    You can add a user to the platform through an invitation. This invitation is sent to them through email as a link. When they click on this link, they will be redirected to a registration form on the platform, where they can fill in their details and are automatically added.

    The details of sending an invitation is shown below:

    1. Click the invite user button
    2. Fill in the user's email in the box and click the 'invite user' button.

    Activate and Deactivate a User #back to top

    Deactivating a user will disable them from logging into the platform. The opposite is true for activating a user.
    To deactivate a user, click on the red button. Click on the button again (now green) to activate the user.

    How to Add a Client #back to top

    Clients can be included to a team/project so that they can view the progress of their work. To do so, you can send them an invitation link through email, inviting them to register on the platform by filling in their details.

    The procedure is shown below:

    1. Click the invite client button
    2. Fill in the client's email in the box and click the 'invite user' button.

    Activate and Deactivate a Client #back to top

    Deactivating a client will disable them from logging into the platform, hence they won't be able to view the progress of their projects. Activating a client will enable them to view their project progress.
    To deactivate a client, click on the red button. Click on the button again to activate the client.
